About Us

12+ Years Experience In Managing EPCM Projects

Headquartered in Singapore, T(h)reeTact has 12 years of experience and an outstanding record of projects. We have assisted companies and government organisations in increasing profitability, identifying inefficiencies and evaluating the associated risks related to the operation of building facilities. Our company mission is to provide reliable and quality products and services to help clients achieve their needs and aspirations. Being a diverse team with varied expertise, we draw upon a specialised range of experience to provide high-quality, high-value products and services. As a major player in the high-tech engineering market of the 21st century, T(h)reeTact is committed to maintain exceptional standards as a top-tier company by capitalising on our human competitive advantage and the use of technology for new products while expanding to different markets.

Case Study: Farmily (2022)

What We Have Done

Farmily has approached us with the intent to increase their profitability and tackle the issue of manpower crunch.

In 6 months, we have (1) Increased vegetation output by 4 times, (2) Reduced the need for physical manpower by half and (3) Provided training for employees to use the new solution and technology which has led to optimal growth condition of crops
